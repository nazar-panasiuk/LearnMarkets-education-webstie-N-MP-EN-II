Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to LearnMarkets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information while you access our educational platform and expert-led resources.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal information: name, email address, and any data provided to access educational courses or materials.
  • Learning preferences: courses viewed, topics of interest, and interaction with educational content.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device, and system settings.
  • Interaction data: pages visited, time spent on courses, and resources accessed.
  • Additional information you provide voluntarily, such as feedback or messages to our team of industry experts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize educational content and learning experiences.
  • To respond to inquiries and feedback regarding courses and resources.
  • To improve the platform and implement new educational features.
  • To analyze user interaction for enhancing the learning experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements related to educational services.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • With service providers assisting in delivering educational content or platform functionality.
  • When required by law or regulatory authorities.
  • With others only with your consent.

5. Security of Your Information

  • Data encryption during transmission.
  • Regular security audits and checks.
  • Staff training on secure handling of user information.
  • Access to personal information limited to authorized personnel only.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data if necessary.
  • Opt out of communications related to platform updates or notifications.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve site functionality, personalize learning experiences, and analyze platform usage. Cookie settings can be adjusted through your browser.

8. Links to Third-Party Platforms

Our educational platform may contain links to third-party resources. We do not control their privacy practices and recommend reviewing their policies before sharing any personal information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If such data is provided in error, please contact us for removal.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our educational platform or legal requirements. All updates will be posted on this page.

11. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at learn_markets@gmail.com.
