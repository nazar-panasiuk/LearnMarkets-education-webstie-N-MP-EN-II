Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to LearnMarkets. This cookie policy explains how we use cookies on our educational platform to enhance your learning experience, provide expert-led educational content, and ensure the proper functioning of our website.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help us remember your preferences, improve website performance, and analyze the effectiveness of our educational services.

3. How we use cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Analyzing website traffic and improving user experience on our educational platform.
  • Personalizing educational content, courses, and learning recommendations.
  • Ensuring the proper functioning of website features, such as course enrollment forms and content navigation.
  • Collecting usage statistics to optimize our educational resources and expert-led guidance.

4. Types of cookies

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Functional cookies — to ensure the website works properly and to provide access to educational content.
  • Analytical cookies — to track learner behavior and improve the educational experience.
  • Personalization cookies — to deliver tailored course suggestions and resources based on your learning preferences.

5. Managing cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality or limit access to specific educational features.

6. Cookie security

We take measures to protect information obtained through cookies from unauthorized access. All data is used solely to enhance the educational experience and provide personalized learning pathways.

7. Third-party cookies

We may use third-party services and providers for analytics and platform optimization. These companies may place their own cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

8. Policy updates

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our educational platform or legal requirements. Any updates will be published on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at learn_markets@gmail.com.
